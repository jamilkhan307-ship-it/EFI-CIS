# Privacy Policy (Draft for MVP)

This Privacy Policy explains how the **CIS Portal** collects, uses, and protects your information.

## 1. Data Collection
We collect the following types of information:
- **Account Information**: Name, email, and company department (collected via authentication).
- **Project Data**: Any Customer Input Requirement (CIR/CIS) records, including part descriptions, customer names, and technical data.
- **Audit Logs**: We track status changes, approvals, and rejections for project tracking and accountability.

## 2. Data Storage
Your data is stored securely in **Supabase**, a PostgreSQL database hosted on enterprise-grade infrastructure. Supabase is **GDPR and SOC2 compliant**.

## 3. Data Usage
Data is used exclusively for:
- Managing and tracking CIS/CIR project lifecycles.
- Facilitating communication between initiators, checkers, and approvers.
- Generating project reports (PDF/Excel).

## 4. Security
We use **Row Level Security (RLS)** in our database to ensure that users can only access the data they are authorized to see based on their roles (Initiator, Checker, etc.).

## 5. Third-Party Services
- **Supabase**: Used for database and authentication.
- **Vercel/Cloudflare**: Used for hosting the application.
- **GitHub**: Used for codebase management and CI/CD.

## 6. Your Rights
As this is a company-internal tool, your data is managed by the company's IT administrator. For any data requests, please contact your internal admin.
